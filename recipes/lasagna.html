Ingredients

  • Flat pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Minced meat
  • Cheese yummy!

Steps

  1. Put pasta in dish
  2. Add minced meat with tomatoes
  3. Add cheese on top of it
  4. Repeat steps to make a tower
  5. Put in the oven
  6. Done
